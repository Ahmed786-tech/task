Slack Notifications

Slack is a popular workplace communication app used across multiple platforms by individuals, teams, and companies to share files, coordinate meetings and overall help projects flow seamlessly.

Being extremely widespread among today’s workforce, its integration within Uni Board is essential and receiving these notifications within the program will undoubtedly increase productivity. Additionally, less switching between devices can lead to less stress and less possible distraction.

Notifications can communicate which channel messages were received within, incoming video calls and more. We’ve made a strong effort on the details to reduce project bottlenecks, communication delays and ideally improve workplace productivity. The integration of this successful and still growing platform can streamline your project goals and improve your team’s efficiency.
